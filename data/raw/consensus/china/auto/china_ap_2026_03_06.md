title: China sets a lower economic growth target of 4.5% to 5% for 2026 as challenges loom

Public reporting around the annual government work report stressed that China lowered its growth target range for 2026 and acknowledged ongoing domestic challenges, especially weak consumption and a fragile property backdrop. The narrative emphasized slower but still positive growth, subdued inflation pressure, and a policy stance that remains supportive as officials try to stabilize expectations and boost domestic demand.
